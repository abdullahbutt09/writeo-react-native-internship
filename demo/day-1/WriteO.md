# 1. What does Writeo do?

**Writeo** is a novel writing and story planning platform designed to help writers create, organize, and manage their stories from one place. It provides tools for planning novels, developing characters, organizing chapters, managing story notes, and tracking writing progress, making the entire writing process more structured and efficient.

---

# 2. Who are the target users of Writeo?

Writeo is primarily designed for:

- Novelists
- Fiction Writers
- Authors
- Story Planners
- Creative Writers

These users can use Writeo to plan, organize, and manage every aspect of their writing projects within a single platform.

---

# 3. Five Major Features of Writeo

## 1. Chapters & Novel Writing

Allows writers to create, edit, and organize chapters while building complete novels.

## 2. Character Management

Enables users to create detailed character profiles, including descriptions, personalities, and other important information.

## 3. Relationship Map

Provides a visual way to manage and understand relationships between different characters in a story.

## 4. Novel Notes

Allows writers to store ideas, research, and important notes related to their stories in one organized location.

## 5. Writing Progress Tracking

Helps writers monitor their writing progress and stay motivated while completing their projects.

---

# 4. Which part of the platform did you find most interesting?

The **Story Creation** feature was the most interesting part of the platform. It allows users to create and organize their own stories from scratch while keeping all related information, such as chapters, characters, and notes, connected within a single workspace. This makes the writing process more organized and easier to manage.

---

# 5. Which part of the platform was confusing at first?

The most confusing part was deleting a character relationship. Initially, I expected the relationship to be removable directly from the relationship view. However, I discovered that I first needed to open the **Character Profile**, navigate to the **Relationships** section, and then delete the relationship from there. Once I understood the workflow, it became straightforward, but it was not immediately obvious during my first experience using the platform.

---

# Conclusion

Writeo provides a well-organized environment for writers to plan and develop their stories. Its character management, relationship mapping, chapter organization, and progress tracking features make it a comprehensive platform for novel writing. Although there was a small learning curve when managing character relationships, the overall user experience became intuitive and enjoyable after exploring the available features.

Overall, Writeo offers a productive workspace for writers by bringing story planning, character development, note-taking, and writing progress into a single platform, making it easier to stay organized throughout the writing process.
